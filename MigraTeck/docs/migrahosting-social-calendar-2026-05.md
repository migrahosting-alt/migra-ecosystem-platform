# MigraHosting Social Calendar

## Window

- Schedule period: May 1, 2026 through May 20, 2026
- Time zone: America/New_York
- Primary goal: keep MigraHosting visible with a steady mix of launch-offer, trust, and dedicated-VPS positioning

## Brand Guardrails

- Keep the promise tight: premium website launch, dedicated VPS, real support, domain and email setup
- Do not use unsupported claims like `Plans from $29/mo`, `up to 75% off`, or generic shared-hosting language
- When possible, keep the sign-off as `Powered by MigraTeck`
- Prefer `https://migrahosting.com/services` for website-launch posts
- Use `https://migrahosting.com/request-access?product=migrahosting` for VPS interest and reviewed provisioning
