MigraDrive Support

MigraDrive is secure cloud storage by MigraTeck and MigraHosting. This support page covers account access, paid plans, storage usage, file transfers, sync and backup issues, deletion requests, and technical help.

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Support Contact

support@migradrive.com

Use the email address tied to your MigraDrive account when contacting support so login, plan, storage, and deletion requests can be verified faster.

Email Support Account Deletion

Access

Login and account access

Contact support if you cannot sign in, are locked out of your account, need help confirming account ownership, or need guidance accessing a workspace after a billing or organization change.

Plans

Billing and paid plans

Support is available for subscription questions, invoice issues, storage upgrades, plan renewals, and cases where account access or quotas are affected by billing state.

Capacity

Storage usage and quotas

Reach out for help understanding storage usage, cleanup options, quota limits, missing available space after deletions, or questions about file version and retention behavior.

Technical support coverage

  • Upload and download issues, including stalled transfers, failed retries, or inconsistent file availability.
  • File sync or backup issues across web, mobile, desktop, or connected workflows.
  • Unexpected file state, preview failures, version history questions, or folder access problems.
  • General technical problems, including errors, abnormal behavior, or service-impacting issues that require investigation.

Account and data deletion

If you need your MigraDrive account or stored data permanently deleted, use the published deletion process. Support can help you understand the process, required verification steps, retention windows, and what is removed versus retained for legal or billing reasons.
